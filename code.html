Booking & Ticket Policies

Birthday Room Booking Policies

expand_more

Reservations

To book a birthday room, a non-refundable deposit of 50% is required. The booking includes 2 hours of playtime, a dedicated party host, and standard decorations.

Payment

The remaining balance is due on the day of the party. We accept cash and credit cards.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Cancellations made at least 14 days in advance will receive a credit for a future booking.
  • No refunds for cancellations made within 14 days of the party.
  • Rescheduling is subject to availability and must be requested at least 7 days prior to the original party date.

Guest & Capacity Rules

The birthday package includes up to 15 children. Additional children will be charged per head. A maximum of 30 people (children and adults) are allowed in the party room.

General Ticket Purchase Policies

expand_more

Ticket Validity

Tickets are valid for the date and time specified at purchase. They are non-transferable.

Refunds & Exchanges

All ticket sales are final. We do not offer refunds or exchanges for purchased tickets. In case of unforeseen closure of the center, we will offer a voucher for a future visit. For more details, contact support.

Entry Conditions

  • All children must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Socks are required for all guests entering the play area.
  • No outside food or drinks are allowed, with the exception of birthday cakes for booked parties.
  • PlayKids Linares is not responsible for lost or stolen items.
